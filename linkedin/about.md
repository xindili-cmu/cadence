## Long bio (for the LinkedIn "About" section)
Worldwide, rehabilitation medicine now evolves by the week — but much of textbook theory and clinical practice still runs on ideas from 10, even 20, years ago. It isn't that clinicians don't want the latest; the information gap leaves them unsure how far the field has already moved. And a knowledge gap is ultimately paid for in patient outcomes.

Cadence crosses that gap for you. Every day we high-frequency-screen the newest rehab research and clinical techniques from 50+ top sources worldwide, score each finding with an AI SIGNAL rating, and file it into eight specialties — keeping only what can actually change a clinical prescription. Five minutes a day turns the world's newest clinical evidence into the knowledge you carry through the clinic door, in front of your patient.

Whether you're a therapist, a rehab student or educator, or a strength coach, Cadence keeps your knowledge current with the world. Curated for professionals — not patient-facing.

我们每天从全球 50+ 个顶级信源高频筛选最新的康复研究与临床技术，用 AI 为每项发现打出 SIGNAL 评分，并归入八大专科，只留真正能改变临床处方的干货。每天 5 分钟，把全球最新的临床证据，变成你面对患者时最硬核的知识武装。面向同行，不面向患者。
